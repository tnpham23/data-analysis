About Me

Hello everyone! My name is Thuong Pham and I am a graduate from University of California, Davis with a B.S. in Computational Cognitive Science and a B.A. in Economics. I'm looking for a full-time opportunity as a Data Analyst or Business Analyst.

With a background in the higher education industry, psychological & economic research, and project management, I am enthusiastic to apply my skillset across multiple industries, with a high interest for tech, business/retail, and marketing. My previous employments and professional development include working at the UC Davis Continuing and Professional Education Department, Going Solo, and iXperience. Through these amazing experiences, I've honed my profiency in tools like Python, R, SQL, and Excel and cultivated a passion for creating digestible visualizations to creatively provide data-driven solutions. Additionally, I thrive in high-pressure, competitive environments both as a learner and a leader as demonstrated by my abilities and success as a former director of a university dance team. Through my diverse, unique experiences, I hope to contribute meaningful and insightful impact at your company demonstrated by my statistical and leadership skills.

Feel free to contact me or connect with me on Linkedin regarding any job/networking opportunities, or to simply have a quick chat! I would love to gain insight about your company culture or experiences in the field!
